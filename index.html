About Me

I prefer cats to dogs, (even though I love them both); tea to coffee, and a night in, just as much as a night out. What led me to web-development? Growing up, I've always considered myself as creative. However, I never found the medium of creativity that was right for me. I tried drawing, and that didn't stick. I tried painting, and although I was "Most Improved" in my class, it still wasn't a right fit. I love music, but don't expect me to play anything for you. It wasn't until I was exposed to coding through an internship, that I saw a creative outlet that could work for me. I mean, I'm literally creating and designing my own website, and that's pretty cool!
